#Personality traits
Randomized, one good, one bad - unlocks after a couple of levels
EX: 
Good: 
Gold rush - +5% hit chance if picked gold in previous move
Bond, bonus if adjacent to the same hero two times a row
Group pressure, +1 move if next to two heroes a turn start

Bad: 
Blood sick - -5% if attacking adjacent to a hero with <50% health
Proj fear, dont like standing in line of fire
